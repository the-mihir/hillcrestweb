MEET THE 2023-2024 CANDIDATES FOR STUDENT GOVERNMENT
Hillcrest High School Student Government Elections

Meet the Candidates:

2023-2024 Candidates for Student Government Candidates Video
SENIOR DUES

Package 1— $140.00 money order payable to HILLCREST HS:

Due date December 1st, 2023 Payment includes Yearbook, jersey, senior picnic, and any other senior activities we host based on funds we collect.

Package #2— $140.00 money order payable to HILLCREST HS:

Due date March 28, 2023 Payment includes Cap, gown, tassel, embroidered stole, diploma case, and 8 tickets for you and guests to attend graduation at St. John’s University on June 21, 2024, at 7:00 pm.

YEARBOOK INFORMATION: MR. HERNANDEZ

Senior portraits November 27th to December 1st. Students will receive a slip with their designated time (period) to go downstairs and take their photo.

Baby pictures: Be on the lookout for an email to your DOE email and a QR code to scan to upload your photos. You must include a baby photo and a current photo of yourself.

Superlatives will be by SLC. You will vote for a person for your SLC to be in particular categories such as Best Dressed, Most Athletic, Class Clown, etc.

SENIOR EVENTS
  • SENIOR PROM, June 7th, 2024, Terrace on the Park 6-10pm $135.00 (payments will be collected in the Spring)
  • Yearbook distribution - June 13 periods 7 and 8 in the auditorium
  • Senior Awards Night - June 14 from 5:30-7:00 pm in the auditorium
  • Graduation Rehearsal - June 18 from 1:00-3:00 pm in the auditorium
  • Cap, gown, tassel, stole, and graduation tickets distribution - June 20 from 2:00-5:00pm in the auditorium
  • Graduation - June 21 from 7:00-9:00pm at St. John's University
  • Senior Picnic - June 25 from 12:00-2:00 pm on the school's lawn

Hillcrest is a STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics) school that proudly offers its students a world-class education with a dynamic faculty and challenging curriculum.
