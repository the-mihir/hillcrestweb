Pre-Med School Of Scientific research Stem

PRE-MED

The Pre-Med School of Scientific Research and STEM offers a Small Learning Community with many opportunities for students who are interested in science and medicine. Here are some of the things they provide:

  • AP classes in Biology, Environmental Science, Calculus, Spanish, Computer Science, English Language and Composition, English Literature and Composition, and Chemistry
  • Science classes like Chemistry, Anatomy, Physics, and Med Lab
  • STEM Research Courses at York College
  • STEM Science Research Internship Opportunities
  • CUNY College Now Courses
  • Participation in NYSEF and STEM Matters
  • FutureReady NYC Program

These opportunities can help students learn more about science and medicine, and also prepare them for college and future careers in STEM fields.
